Game Jams

This page collects the games that I have worked on at game Jams. For the uniniated, game jams are events where people gather, either online or in person, and make games in a short period of time, usually between three days and two weeks. Given the time constraints the games are usually not fully polished but are often fun and creative. I suggest you take a look at Scale, Crazy Uber, or Zombie Scrapyard, for some of my most successful game jam games.

Crazy Uber

Crazy Uber is a physics driving game inspired by Crazy Taxi made for TOJam 11.

Winter Wizard Jam

Winter Wizard Jam is a kite powered line rider made for the Winter Wizard Jam.

So it Has Come to This

So it Has Come to This is a post apocalyptic worker placement made for TOJam 10.

After You

After you is a two player psychedelic game of tag made for TOJam 9.

Zombie Scrapyard

Zombie Scrapyard is an (un)cooperative game survival game made for TOJam 8.

Fall

Fall is a gravity twisting puzzle platformer made for TOJam 6.

Mad Science

Mad Science is an action puzzle game all about light made for TOJam 5.

Scale

Scale is a puzzle platformer all about changing your size made for TOJam 4.

Choon!

Choon is a game about mining cheese on the moon and fighting off mice inspired by MotherLoad made for TOJam 3.

Progger

Progger is a physics puzzle game made for the 2008 U of T Game Design and Development Club Game Making Deathmatch.
